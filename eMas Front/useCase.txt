1. Job Management — Create, Read, Update, Delete
UC-J01. Create a new job via form

Actor: Production planner
Goal: Schedule a production job manually with optional split into multiple slots
Steps:

Navigate to Job Scheduling page

Click "Create Schedule" or "Add Job"

Fill in: Product ID/Name, Target Machine(s), Start Date/Time, Duration, Quantity, Priority, Notes

Optionally split job into multiple slots with partial quantities

Click Submit
Expected: Job appears in Gantt chart; slots assigned to machines; system validates no conflicts

UC-J02. Create a job via NLP

Actor: Operator or planner
Goal: Create a job using natural language with slot splitting and step assignment
Steps:

Open AI Assistant chat

Type: "Create a job for 500 units of Valve Body Assembly on CNC Mill 01, split 200 units now and 300 later, deadline April 5, 2026"

Review auto-filled form and suggested split schedule

Submit
Expected: NLP extracts entities → form pre-filled → slot schedule created → user confirms → job created

UC-J03. Read/list all jobs

Actor: Any user
Goal: View all scheduled and historical jobs
Steps:

Navigate to Job Scheduling

View Gantt chart with jobs and their split slots

Scroll/zoom for different time ranges
Expected: All jobs visible with slot-level details; completed steps highlighted; workflow connections shown

UC-J04. Read job details (including split slots)

Actor: Operator, planner
Goal: See full details of a single job, step assignments, and slot schedule
Steps:

Click a job step or slot in Gantt

View Job Details panel with: Job ID, product, total/slot quantities, machine assignments, step sequences, status, deadlines
Expected: Complete job and step-slot information

UC-J05. Update job (reschedule / slot adjustment)

Actor: Planner
Goal: Change job start time, machine, duration, or split slot quantities
Steps:

Select job in Gantt

Modify start date/time, machine, duration, or slot quantity

Save
Expected: Job updated; slots rescheduled; conflict check runs

UC-J06. Update job via NLP

Actor: Operator
Goal: Reschedule or change job using natural language
Steps:

Chat: "Reschedule Job #P-2404 slot 2 to tomorrow morning on CNC Mill 02"

Confirm suggested change
Expected: System updates slot schedule; user notified

UC-J07. Delete/cancel a job

Actor: Planner
Goal: Cancel a job or specific slot
Steps:

Select job/slot

Click "Cancel" or "Delete"

Confirm
Expected: Job/slot cancelled; machine freed

UC-J08. Duplicate a job

Actor: Planner
Goal: Create a similar job for a different date/quantity with step/slot info
Steps:

Select existing job

Click "Duplicate"

Adjust date, quantity, or machine slots

Save
Expected: New job created with step/slot template copied
